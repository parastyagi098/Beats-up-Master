What is BEATS UP?
Beats up provides you online air drums. Air drums is
a revolutionary air-drumming instrument that allows
drumming without a drum set. Created for the musician
who wants to be expressive and versatile while solving the
problems of portability, space and noise associated with
regular drum sets. Air drums is the perfect solution
for drummers playing drums at home or on the go.
